About Us

The Cybercrime Reporting Portal is a secure, government-supported platform created to help citizens across India report cybercrimes easily and confidentially. Our goal is to provide a centralized digital space where victims can raise complaints, upload evidence, and track the progress of their cases—all from the safety of their homes.

We work in close collaboration with:

  • State and Central Law Enforcement Agencies
  • Cybersecurity Experts and IT Professionals
  • Legal Advisors
  • Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) that offer victim support and awareness campaigns

Cybercrimes such as online harassment, financial fraud, identity theft, and data breaches have become increasingly common. This portal empowers individuals—especially women, children, and vulnerable groups—by offering features such as:

  • Easy-to-use interface
  • Anonymity options for sensitive complaints
  • Encrypted storage of evidence and personal information
  • 24/7 access from mobile and desktop devices

Whether you're an individual seeking help, a concerned parent, or a professional protecting your identity online, we are here to support you. We believe that awareness, reporting, and swift action are the keys to a safer digital society.

Together, let's stand against cybercrime and make the internet a safer place for all.
