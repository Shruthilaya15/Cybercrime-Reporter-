Our Services

At the Cybercrime Reporting Portal, we offer a range of services to help you respond to and recover from cyber incidents quickly and safely:

Online Complaint Registration

Report cybercrimes such as online fraud, harassment, hacking, or identity theft with a simple and secure submission process.

Real-Time Case Tracking

Track the status of your complaint using your unique reference ID, with updates at every stage of the investigation.

Digital Evidence Upload

Submit screenshots, emails, transaction details, or other relevant digital evidence to support your complaint.

Cyber Safety Tips & Awareness

Learn how to stay safe online through our awareness resources, guidelines, and alerts about trending scams.

Helpdesk & Support

Get assistance through our dedicated support team for any queries related to complaints, portal usage, or cybercrime awareness.

Report Phishing & Fake Websites

Identify and report suspicious websites, emails, or links that attempt to steal your personal or financial information. Our team works with cybersecurity experts to investigate and take action.
